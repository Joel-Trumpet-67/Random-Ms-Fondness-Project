## Scale A (most categories): 8 / 6 / 4 / 2
## Scale B (Other section): 4 / 3 / 2 / 1

### Express info/opinions (8/6/4/2)
- 8: Authentic, no judgement/disrespect, shows care, eye contact
- 6: Authentic strategies used
- 4: Not authentic OR disrespectful/judgmental
- 2: Not authentic AND disrespectful

### Clear positive message (8/6/4/2)
- 8: Clear, positive, health-enhancing
- 6: Clear, positive, health-enhancing (slight difference from 8)
- 4: Positive, health-enhancing but not clear
- 2: Positive but does not enhance health

### Compassion & conviction (8/6/4/2)
- 8: Effectively encourages with compassion AND conviction
- 6: Encourages with compassion
- 4: Encourages but without compassion
- 2: Only advises

### Coping strategies (8/6/4/2)
- 8: 5+ strategies
- 6: 4 strategies
- 4: 3 strategies
- 2: 1 strategy

### Sources of help (8/6/4/2)
- 8: 4+ sources
- 6: 3 sources
- 4: 2 sources
- 2: 1 source

### Concepts (8/6/4/2)
- 8: 10+ concepts, accurate, shows relationships
- 6: 8+ concepts, accurate, shows relationships
- 4: <7 concepts OR not accurate OR no relationships shown
- 2: Not accurate AND no relationships shown

### Length (4/3/2/1)
- 4: 2-3 minutes
- 3: Under 2 min OR over 3 min
- 2: Under 1 min OR over 4 min
- 1: (implied)

### Script use (4/3/2/1)
- 4: Knows it so well they don't need it
- 3: Glances at it
- 2: Relies heavily / occasionally reads
- 1: Reads directly from script
